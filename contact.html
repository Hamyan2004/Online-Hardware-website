Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI recommendation system work?
Our AI system analyzes your project requirements, skill level, budget, and preferences to provide personalized tool recommendations. It learns from your interactions and improves suggestions over time.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy for unused items in original packaging. Tools that have been used can be returned within 7 days if they don't meet your expectations.
Do you offer technical support?
Yes! Our technical support team is available 24/7 to help you with product questions, installation guidance, and troubleshooting. Contact us via phone, email, or live chat.
How long does shipping take?
Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days, while express shipping delivers within 1-2 business days. We also offer same-day delivery in select areas.
Do you offer bulk discounts?
Yes, we offer special pricing for bulk orders and contractor accounts. Contact our sales team for custom pricing based on your volume requirements.
